Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 20, 2026

Overview

Vīra ("the App") is a workout tracking application. Your privacy is fundamental to how the App is built. The App does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. All data remains on your device.

Data Collection

The App does not collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising identifiers
  • Usage analytics or telemetry
  • Health data via HealthKit or any health framework

Data Storage

All workout data is stored locally on your device using an on-device SQLite database. This data is not accessible to us or any third party.

Data Sharing

The App does not share any data with third-party analytics services, advertising networks, social media platforms, or any external servers.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party services that collect user data. There are no ads, no tracking SDKs, and no external API calls.

Data Import

The App allows you to import workout data from CSV files stored on your device. This import is processed entirely on-device. No data is uploaded or transmitted.

Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time from Settings → Delete All Data. If you delete the App, all data is permanently removed.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any data from children under 13. Since the App collects no data from any user, it is compliant with COPPA.

Changes

If we update this policy, changes will be reflected on this page. Since the App collects no data, we do not anticipate significant changes.
