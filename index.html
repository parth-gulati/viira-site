Vīra

Your workout data, your way.

Vīra is a workout tracker built for people who care about their data. Track exercises, log sets, monitor PRs, and visualize your progress — all stored locally on your device.

Features

  • Workout Tracking — Exercises, sets, weight, reps, tempo, supersets
  • 100+ Exercises — Full library with instructions and muscle group filters
  • Templates — Preset and custom workout templates
  • PR Detection — Automatic personal records with 1RM estimation
  • Analytics — Heatmap, PR trends, volume/TUT charts, effort scores
  • Calorie Estimation — MET-based calorie burn per workout
  • Cardio Support — Duration and distance tracking for cardio exercises
  • Streaks — Daily or weekly streak tracking
  • History — Weekly grouping with volume bars and PR badges
  • Import — Bring your data from other apps via CSV
  • Privacy First — No accounts, no cloud, no tracking

Privacy

Vīra does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All workout data lives on your device. No analytics, no tracking, no third-party services. Full privacy policy →

FAQ

Is Vīra free?

Yes, completely free. No ads, no subscriptions.

Where is my data stored?

Everything is stored locally on your device. Nothing leaves your phone.

Can I import from another app?

Yes. Settings → Import from CSV. Supports standard workout exports with 170+ exercise name mappings.

Will there be cloud sync?

We're considering it for a future update. Your existing features will always remain free.
